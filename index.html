Welcome to my website; I am a Creative Technologist in Saint Louis, Missouri.

My rather unconventional background includes a diverse range of experience; a curious series of events led me to where I am today. I am truly a "jack of all trades" in every respect.

My intrinsic creativity is now tightly interwoven with a newfound passion for the technical. Highly imaginitive by nature, I tend to thrive where ingenuity is valued and encouraged.

My introduction to the realm of digital art began nearly twenty years ago, reproducing vector-based logos for a small promotional products company. Later, as co-owner of that same business, graphic design became an integral part of my full-time duties.

This often leaves people a bit baffled, but the vast majority of my professional experience was in law enforcement.

One evening, during an otherwise quiet shift, that career came to an abrupt end; I had been a police officer for over ten years.

Three surgeries and months of physical therapy later, we'll just say "the ability to leap tall buildings in a single bound" is no longer part of my skill set.

"When one door closes,

another opens..."

- Alexander Grahm Bell

So there I was, in my early forties, confronted with a question of "what to be when I grow up" for the second time.

By pure happenstance, I discovered our local LaunchCode program while watching some local-interest programming on PBS one evening. After attending several meet-ups and exploring the various options, I began charting a course toward my new career path in web development.
